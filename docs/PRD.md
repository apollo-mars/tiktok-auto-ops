# Product Requirements Document for Vietnam TikTok Shop

## Overview
This PRD outlines the requirements and architecture for launching the TikTok Shop in Vietnam, focusing on 3C accessories while ensuring compliance with local regulations.

## Compliance-First Approach
We will adopt a compliance-first strategy to ensure that all aspects of the TikTok Shop adhere to local laws and regulations concerning e-commerce and consumer rights.

## Architecture
The system architecture will be designed to facilitate scalability and reliability, incorporating modular components that can be independently developed and deployed.

## MVP Milestones
- Research local market trends
- Define key product categories
- Implement basic shopping cart functionality
- Launch a marketing campaign to generate awareness

## Non-Goals
- Full integration with all logistics providers initially
- Support for multiple languages at launch

## Acceptance Criteria
- Successful launch of the shop by [insert date]
- Achievement of [insert sales target] within the first quarter after launch.
